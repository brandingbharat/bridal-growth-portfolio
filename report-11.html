04. Quality vs. Quantity Framework

The eternal dilemma: post daily to please the algorithm, or post weekly to maintain premium standards? For a salon charging premium rates, Trust > Reach.

The Golden Ratio

70% Quality : 30% Quantity

  • 70% High Polish: Professional camera, planned lighting, final bridal looks.
  • 30% Raw/Real: Phone BTS, quick tips, stories, candid moments.

"A bad photo stays on your grid forever. A missed posting day is forgotten tomorrow."
