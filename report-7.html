1. The "Verification Engine"

In Jaipur, Instagram is for inspiration, but Facebook is for trust. Families and financial decision-makers (Mothers, Buas) use Facebook to verify a salon's credibility before booking. While Instagram captures the bride, Facebook captures the family paying the bill.

2. Who are we Targeting?

The Jaipur market is segmented by budget and decision power. The "Smart-Value Bride" and "Budget Bride" represent the largest volume, while the "Premium Bride" is a niche segment. Crucially, the "Mother/Decision Maker" segment controls the final booking.

3. High-Impact Content Strategy

Not all content is created equal. In Jaipur, Video Content (long-form tutorials, transformations) and structured Facebook Albums drive the highest reach and conversion. Text posts have minimal impact. Authenticity beats aesthetics—"Real Bride" photos perform better than models.
